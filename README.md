# Sparkle — Luxury Cosmetics

Sparkle is an online beauty boutique for skincare, makeup, and body care products, wrapped in an elegant black, ivory, and rose-gold look.

**When you land on the site**, you're greeted with a rotating showcase of featured products and a "Shop Now" button, plus a banner up top letting you know about current deals (like extra discounts and free shipping over $50).

**Browsing products** is easy — you can filter by category (Skincare, Makeup, or Body Care), search by name, and sort by price, rating, or what's newest. Each product shows its photo, price, star rating, and reviews, with little badges for "New" or "Bestseller" items. Clicking any product opens a bigger, detailed view with the full description before you decide to buy.

**Not sure what to pick?** There's a "Vanity Matchmaker" quiz that asks a few questions about your skin and preferences, then suggests products that suit you.

**Shopping** works the way you'd expect: heart an item to save it to your wishlist for later (it'll still be there next time you visit), add things to your cart, and adjust quantities before checking out. Checkout walks you through shipping details, then payment (card, UPI, or PayPal-style), with helpful error messages if something's missing or looks off, and ends with an order confirmation.

There's also a brand story section to learn about Sparkle, a newsletter signup, and a footer with useful links.

**Behind the scenes**, there's a private admin area where the store owner can add, edit, or remove products — but it's intentionally hidden from regular visitors and only reachable with a secret keyboard shortcut and password.
